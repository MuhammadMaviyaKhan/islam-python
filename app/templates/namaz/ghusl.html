Ghusl (Ritual Bath)

Ghusl Janabat (Ritual Impurity)

Ghusl Janabat is obligatory after sexual intercourse or seminal discharge, required before prayer, fasting, and touching Quranic text.

Method (based on Shia fiqh from Tahrir al-Wasilah of Imam Khomeini):

  1. Niyyah: Intend to perform Ghusl Janabat for the pleasure of Allah
  2. Tartibi Method (Sequential): Wash the head and neck first, then the right side of the body, then the left side
  3. Irtimasi Method (Immersion): Immerse the entire body in water (pool, river, sea) at once with intention
Ghusl Jummah (Friday)

A recommended (Mustahab) Ghusl performed on Fridays before Zuhr prayer. Method is the same as Ghusl Janabat.

Other Mustahab Ghusls
  • Eid ul-Fitr and Eid ul-Adha
  • Day of Arafah (9th Dhul-Hijjah)
  • Night of Qadr (19th, 21st, 23rd Ramadan)
  • Day of Ashura (10th Muharram)
  • Entering the Haram of Makkah or Madinah
