Tayammum (Dry Ablution)

When Tayammum is Permitted
  • When water is unavailable
  • When water would cause harm to health
  • When water is insufficient for Wudu/Ghusl
  • When time for prayer is too short to use water
Method (Based on Shia Fiqh)
  1. Niyyah: Intend to perform Tayammum for the pleasure of Allah
  2. First Strike: Strike both palms on pure earth/dust
  3. Wipe Forehead: Wipe the forehead from the hairline to the bridge of the nose
  4. Wipe Right Hand: Wipe the back of the right hand from wrist to fingertips
  5. Wipe Left Hand: Wipe the back of the left hand similarly
  6. Note: The surface used must be pure earth, sand, stone, or dust
Tayammum is invalidated by the same things that invalidate Wudu. It also becomes invalid when water becomes available.
