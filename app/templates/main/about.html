About Shia Islam

Shia Islam is one of the two main branches of Islam, followed by approximately 10-15% of Muslims worldwide. The term "Shia" comes from "Shiat Ali" (followers of Ali). Shia Muslims believe that after the death of Prophet Muhammad (SAWW), divine leadership (Imamate) was entrusted to Imam Ali (AS) and his descendants from the Ahlul Bayt.

Core Beliefs
  • Tawhid: Oneness of Allah
  • Adl: Divine Justice
  • Nubuwwah: Prophethood
  • Imamah: Divine leadership of the twelve Imams (AS)
  • Yawm al-Qiyamah: Day of Resurrection
Primary Sources
  • The Holy Quran
  • Al-Kafi (by Sheikh al-Kulayni)
  • Man La Yahduruhu al-Faqih (by Sheikh al-Saduq)
  • Tahdhib al-Ahkam and Al-Istibsar (by Sheikh al-Tusi)
  • Nahjul Balagha (sermons of Imam Ali AS)
  • Sahifa Sajjadiya (prayers of Imam Ali Zainul Abideen AS)

This website aims to provide authentic resources for Shia Muslims worldwide, including Duas, Ziyarat, Namaz guides, and Islamic knowledge based on authentic sources.
